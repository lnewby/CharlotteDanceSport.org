In the past, we have had many people request housing, and only a few people utilize the housing provided by our team. We have decided to ask for a $10 down payment, per person, for housing to encourage people to utilize their housing arrangements. The money is non-refundable, and will be considered a donation towards scholarships at CDC 2012.

Even though we try to get visiting teams sleeping space, we recommend getting a hotel room to share. Hotels near campus are very affordable so instead of traveling 30 minutes to the host house, it might be worth sharing a room with your friends.
